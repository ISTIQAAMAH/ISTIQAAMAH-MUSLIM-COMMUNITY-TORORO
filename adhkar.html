Morning & Evening Adhkar

Morning Adhkar

SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, La ilaha illallah, Allahu Akbar. Recite after Fajr for protection and blessings throughout the day.

Evening Adhkar

Astaghfirullah, La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah, Ayat Al-Kursi, recite after Maghrib to safeguard yourself and your family through the night.
