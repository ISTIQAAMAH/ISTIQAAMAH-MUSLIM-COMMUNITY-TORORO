Our Community Events

At Istiqaamah Muslim Community – Tororo, we organize events that bring our members together, strengthen Islamic knowledge, and serve the wider community. From educational seminars and youth leadership programs to Ramadan activities and health outreaches, each event is designed to foster faith, unity, and service. Whether you are joining an upcoming event or exploring past initiatives, we welcome everyone to participate and benefit from the knowledge and experience shared.
