Our Mission

To nurture a united Muslim community grounded in the Qur’an and Sunnah, promoting worship, education, service, moral guidance, and community development for Allah's sake.

Our Vision

To be a model Islamic community in Uganda that inspires righteousness, strengthens faith, and shapes future generations with knowledge, discipline, and strong Islamic identity.
